# Attribution

Every third-party asset used in Extermino must be credited here with its name,
source URL, author, and license.

## 3D Models

_No external model assets yet — trees are currently procedural placeholders.
GLB tree models (oak, birch) will be added from CC0 / CC-BY sources and credited
here._

## Textures

_No external textures yet — terrain uses a flat material placeholder. PBR grass
texture sets (albedo, normal, roughness, AO) will be added from
[Poly Haven](https://polyhaven.com/textures) (CC0) and credited here._

## Sounds & Music

_No audio assets yet — the audio system loads from `public/sounds/` if present.
Ambient tracks (birds, wind, distant stream, looping music) will be added from
CC0 sources ([Freesound](https://freesound.org), [Pixabay](https://pixabay.com/music/))
and credited here._
